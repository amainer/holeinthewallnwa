What We're About

This cave and 12 acres is remembered fondly by Bella Vista, AR locals. Used in the 1930’s by farmers for hay storage, later for fiddling and square dancing, visited secretly by lovers and mischief makers, and offered up as a sanctuary for wildlife, this piece of Bella Vista history is now available for all to enjoy. The Hole in the Wall NWA is a space for mountain bikers, locals, families, and groups to step out of the rat race, step back in time, and connect with nature, friends, family, and community. While this destination feels a world away, we're located just 18 minutes away from Downtown Bentonville's many breweries, restaurants, trails, special events, and the fabulous Crystal Bridges museum.

Are You Ready?

Ride

We’re lucky to have year-round riding in Northwest Arkansas and The Hole in the Wall NWA is perfectly located for those visiting the region to ride. From the foot of your campsite to the trailhead, you’re 10-20 minutes from hundreds of miles of professionally designed terrain in the Oz Trails system, including The Back 40, Coler, Slaughter Pen, Blowing Springs, and more. As avid outdoor folk and mountain bikers ourselves, we're happy to help you plan a perfect solo trek, or find guides and groups to ride with. In October 2019, Hole in the Wall will be a stone’s throw from the 50-mile trail expansion planned for Bella Vista - more to come!

Rally

After a day out-and-about, it’s time to celebrate the end of another great day in the Ozarks. If you want to stay on-site, go on and grab a cool beverage, take a seat, and relax in the cave or around the fire with friends and family. Let the kids run around the open field playing the games you remember from your childhood, before smartphones were a thing. Looking for more of a scene? Downtown Bentonville is just down the road with food trucks, fine dining, local breweries and craft cocktails. A rotating lineup of live music and events gather the community for culture and fun almost every week of the year. If you’re looking for a day off from riding, there is plenty to do - catch an exhibition at The Crystal Bridges Museum, take a hike, hit the water, and much more. Just ask us for recommendations!

Rest & Repeat

The Hole in the Wall NWA is initially offering primitive camping with tent platforms, fire rings, and porta-pottys. Those with RVs or vans that don’t require hookups are welcome. Our start on amenities and accommodations is rustic, but we’ve got plenty more exciting options planned for the future, including tiny cabins, live music, local fare and more. We'll be posting our master plan soon, so check back for a sneak peak. Y’all come back now, y’hear?
